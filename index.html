The land of Prominence
A mystical land of towering castles, enchanted forests, and ancient ruins, where sword-wielding knights and powerful sorcerers clash. Kingdoms battle for power, while magical creatures and forgotten gods shape the world. Magic flows through the land, influencing the fate of all who live here.

The Primary Classes

Warrior

A durable melee fighter focused on physical damage and tanking, excelling in close combat and defense.

Priest

A support class that heals, buffs allies, and uses holy or dark magic for protection and damage.

Rogue

A stealthy, agile class specializing in quick, surprise attacks and evasion, often dealing high damage with stealth.

Mage

A spellcaster who uses powerful magic to deal damage, control the battlefield, and summon mystical forces.
